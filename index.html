Briefly

Easypay makes shopping so easy.With just the QR code of the supermarket, customers are now able to make transactions by scanning the code and choosing their items of preference then proceeding to order for them by just clicking on them. Very interesting!!...Then with mobile money or visa payment the customers are abkle to pay for their products.

How It Works

The short video below shows how the easypay mobile app works. It's very easy and convinient to use.

Steps

Registration and Log In
This is the initial step and a user is required to first do registration. A valid email address is required for a successful registration, then one can therefore successfully login in with there already set password.

Scanning
This is arguably the most important step of the all where the already logged in user is therefore in position to scan the Qr code of the supermarket. the available products then can show and one can proceed to choose the products they would like to buy at the particular time.

Payment
After completion of the first two steps then one is able is make the payment. There are two means of payment; visa card payment and mobile money payment.
