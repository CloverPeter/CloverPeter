Briefly

Easypay makes shopping so easy.With just the QR code of the supermarket, customers are now able to make transactions by scanning the code and choosing their items of preference then proceeding to order for them by just clicking on them. Very interesting!!...Then with mobile money or visa payment the customers are abkle to pay for their products.
